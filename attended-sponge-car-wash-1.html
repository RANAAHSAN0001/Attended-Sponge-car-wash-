Our Story

Built on hand-washing, scratch-free care, and Dublin's trust

Attended Sponge Car Wash started in Greenhills with one goal: give Dublin drivers the kind of care their car actually deserves. No automated brushes, no rushed jobs — just a dedicated team, soft microfibre tools, and pH-balanced premium products used correctly, every single time.

Every wash is done entirely by hand using scratch-free techniques, so your paintwork stays protected wash after wash. Our friendly, experienced staff treat every car — from the family hatchback to the weekend supercar — with the same level of attention to detail.
